Cavetown

...or otherwise known as Robin Daniel Skinner or Robbie is an English singer-songwriter, record producer and youtuber. His music usually uses elements of indie rock, indie pop and bedroom pop with mellow, gentle ukelele ballads. On Spotify, he has a monthly streamers count of 6.6 million. His Youtube channel also has roughly 2.21 million subscribers and 534 million views.


▷ Early Life

Robbie was born in Oxford, England, on the 15th of December in 1998. His interest in music was piqued by his father who was a musicologist and choir director. He learned how to play the guitar from his father at the age of 8. His mother was also in the field of music as a profession Baroque flautist and a music teacher. He was raised in Cambridge and went to Parkside Community College from 2010 to 2015 then Hills Road Sixth Form College until 2017.


▷ Personal Life

  • Robbie has publicly come out as transgender (using he/they pronouns) and being on both aromantic and asexual spectrums in September of 2020.
  • Robbie, as of 2019, lives in Cambridge.
  • He also has an attention deficit hyperactivity disorder.
  • Skinner also has a cat named Juno. One of his songs in his album worm food is about her.
