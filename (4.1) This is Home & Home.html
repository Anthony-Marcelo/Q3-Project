This is Home & Home

▷ 0:01/8:16 |-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------| ⛶

Two songs with almost identical lyrics but completely different vibes. "This is Home" was the first to release of the two, being released way back in 2015. It brought along with it a more calming, bedroom pop sound. It was the more gentle of the two, a comfort song if you will. Meanwhile, "Home" released a whole 4 years later in 2019, brings a whole different kind of feeling along with it. Robbie's voice is deeper, signifying the passing of time. He also portrays the story with stronger emotions than he did the first time around. Overall, both of these songs hit very close to home for me and I find comfort in listening to both alternatingly.
